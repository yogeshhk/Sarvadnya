Paul Adelstein is an American actor known for the role of Agent Paul Kellerman in the Fox television series Prison Break and his role as pediatrician Cooper Freedman in the ABC medical drama Private Practice . In addition to supporting roles in films such as Intolerable Cruelty and Memoirs of a Geisha he is also known for his recurring role as Leo Bergen on ABC s Scandal and as Jake Novak in the Bravo television series Girlfriends Guide to Divorce .
